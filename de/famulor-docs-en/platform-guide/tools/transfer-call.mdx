## Warm Call Transfer

Famulor's Warm Call Transfer feature enables true warm transfers with hold, briefing, and seamless connection.

### Configuration

When configuring Warm Call Transfer, you can set:

- **Supervisor Phone Number**: The number to dial for the transfer (supports SIP addresses/extensions)
- **Outbound Phone Number**: The number used to dial the supervisor (must have outbound trunk)
- **Caller ID**: What number the supervisor sees (e.g., customer's phone number)
- **Hold Music**: Audio played to caller while on hold
- **Hold Music Volume**: Volume level (0-100%)
- **When to Transfer**: Conditions that trigger the transfer
- **Hold Message**: Message spoken before placing caller on hold
- **Summary Instructions**: How to brief the supervisor (who, why, context)
- **Briefing Initial Message**: First message to supervisor when they answer
- **Connected Message**: Message to caller after connection is established

### Benefits

- Context preserved for agents
- Professional hold experience with music
- Works with web calls and SIP trunks without REFER support
