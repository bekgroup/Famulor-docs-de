# Voice Cloning Contract Templates

## 8. Practical Implementation

### 8.1 Obtaining and Documenting Consent

#### Legal Basis

In Germany, Austria, and Switzerland, the **voice is recognized as personal and protected data**. Voice cloning without explicit consent can have significant legal consequences.

<Warning>
Consent is **mandatory** for the legally compliant use of voice cloning.
</Warning>

#### Requirements for Consent

<Steps>
  <Step title="Voluntariness">
    Consent must be given voluntarily, informed, and explicitly
  </Step>
  <Step title="Documentation">
    Written or electronic documentation is required
  </Step>
  <Step title="Purpose Limitation">
    Clear definition of purpose, scope, and duration of use
  </Step>
  <Step title="Right of Withdrawal">
    Revocable at any time with clear consequences
  </Step>
</Steps>

#### Best Practices

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Transparency" icon="eye">
    Clear information about purpose and scope of voice usage
  </Card>
  <Card title="Proof of Consent" icon="file-text">
    Careful archiving and protection against unauthorized access
  </Card>
  <Card title="Protection of Minors" icon="child">
    Consent from legal guardians is required
  </Card>
  <Card title="Labeling" icon="tag">
    Identifiability for users and third parties for synthetic voices
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Sample Contracts

### Germany: Consent and Usage Agreement

<Accordion title="Complete Sample Contract for Germany">

#### Consent and Usage Agreement for Voice Cloning (Germany)

**Contracting Parties**

**Speaker:**  
- Name: ______________________________  
- Address: ___________________________

**Client:**  
- Name/Company: _______________________  
- Address: ___________________________

---

**1. Subject of the Contract**

The subject of this contract is the use of the speaker’s voice for the production of voice recordings and their use within voice cloning and artificial intelligence.

**2. Scope of Rights Transfer**

The use of the voice, its modulation, timbre, associated gestures, and all comparable features of the speaker is **permitted exclusively for the specific purpose defined in this contract** and the concrete production.

<Warning>
The use of the voice for input (including text and data mining), archiving, training, simulation, or other activities related to AI, machine learning, or voice cloning is **not permitted without explicit written consent**.
</Warning>

**3. Consent and Remuneration**

- Use of the voice for AI applications (including voice cloning) requires **explicit, written consent**  
- A **separate, appropriate remuneration** for use within AI applications must be agreed upon  
- Remuneration: ________________________ €

**4. Data Protection (GDPR-compliant)**

Processing of voice recordings is done in accordance with GDPR. The voice is considered **personal biometric data** and may only be processed with consent.

**Rights of the Speaker:**  
- Right to information about stored data  
- Right to deletion ("right to be forgotten")  
- Right to object to data processing

**5. Withdrawal and Deletion**

Consent to the use of the voice for AI applications can be **withdrawn at any time with future effect**.

In case of withdrawal, the client commits to:  
- Immediate deletion of all stored voice data  
- Deletion of all derived AI models  
- Ceasing any further use

**6. Purpose Limitation**

Purpose of use: ___________________

Use is **strictly purpose-bound** and must not be used for other purposes.

**7. Final Provisions**

- Amendments require **written form**  
- If individual provisions are invalid, the remainder of the contract remains effective  
- **German law** applies

**Signature Speaker:** ______________________

**Signature Client:** ______________________

</Accordion>

### Austria: GDPR-Compliant Template

<Accordion title="Sample Contract for Austria">

#### Consent and Usage Agreement for Voice Cloning (Austria)

**Between**

**Provider:** [Name and address of the company]

**and**

**User:** [Name and address of the data subject]

---

**1. Subject of the Agreement**

The user consents to the provider recording, processing, and creating an AI model that simulates the voice by means of **technical procedures (voice cloning)**. 

Processing is carried out in accordance with the **GDPR** and the **Austrian Data Protection Act**.

**2. Purpose of Use**

The cloned voice will be used exclusively for the following purposes:

☐ Training of AI systems  
☐ Creation of voice recordings for: _________________  
☐ Others: ____________________________

**3. Type and Scope of Use**

- Use **exclusively** for the purposes mentioned above  
- Disclosure to third parties only with **explicit consent**  
- Commercial use: ☐ Yes ☐ No

**4. Storage Duration**

- Voice recordings and AI model will be stored for ____________  
- Deletion will occur after expiration, unless statutory retention obligations exist

**5. Right of Withdrawal**

<Info>
The user can withdraw this consent **at any time without stating reasons** with future effect.
</Info>

**Consequences of Withdrawal:**  
- Immediate deletion of voice recordings  
- Deletion of the AI model  
- Cessation of all further processing

**6. GDPR Rights**

The user has the following rights:  
- **Right to information** (Art. 15 GDPR)  
- **Right to deletion** (Art. 17 GDPR)  
- **Right to rectification** (Art. 16 GDPR)  
- **Right to data portability** (Art. 20 GDPR)

**7. Data Security**

The provider commits to **appropriate technical and organizational measures** to protect the data.

**8. Liability**

- Liability only in cases of **intent or gross negligence**  
- For damages caused by unauthorized third-party use, only if duty of care is proven breached

**9. Applicable Law**

**Austrian law** applies. Place of jurisdiction is [Location].

---

**Place, Date:** ____________________

**Signature Provider:** ____________________

**Signature User:** ____________________

</Accordion>

### Switzerland: Data Protection Compliant

<Accordion title="Sample Contract for Switzerland">

#### Consent and Usage Agreement for Voice Cloning (Switzerland)

**Between**

**Responsible Entity:** [Name and address]

**and**

**Data Subject:** [Name and address]

---

**1. Subject of the Contract**

The data subject consents that the responsible entity records, processes, and creates an AI model for voice imitation using **voice cloning technology**.

**2. Legal Basis**

Processing is carried out according to:  
- **Swiss Data Protection Act (DSG)**  
- **Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP)**  
- EU adequacy decision for cross-border processing

**3. Purposes of Use**

☐ AI system training  
☐ Voice recordings for: _______________  
☐ Other purposes: ___________________

**4. Scope of Processing**

- **Exclusive use** for defined purposes  
- Third-party transfer only with **explicit approval**  
- **Commercial exploitation:** ☐ Yes ☐ No

**5. Data Retention**

- Retention period: ________________  
- **Automatic deletion** after expiration  
- Exception: statutory retention obligations

**6. Right of Withdrawal**

<Warning>
**Right of withdrawal at any time** without justification with immediate future effect.
</Warning>

**Consequences of Withdrawal:**  
- Immediate cessation of processing  
- Deletion of all voice recordings  
- Destruction of the AI model

**7. Data Subject Rights under DSG**

- **Right to access** processed data  
- **Right to rectification** of incorrect data  
- **Right to deletion** in case of unlawful processing  
- **Right to object** to processing

**8. Data Security**

Commitment to **appropriate technical and organizational protective measures** according to the state of the art.

**9. Cross-Border Data Transfers**

- Transfers only to **countries with adequate data protection levels**  
- EU countries covered by adequacy decision  
- Third countries only with additional guarantees

**10. Liability Provisions**

- Liability for **intentional or grossly negligent** harm  
- Exclusion of liability for **force majeure**  
- Insurance coverage for data protection breaches

**11. Applicable Law**

**Swiss law** applies. Competent court: [Location].

## Tax Aspects

### Income Tax

<Info>
**License fees** are generally subject to **income tax** in Germany, Austria, and Switzerland.
</Info>

#### Cross-Border Licenses

With international license agreements, **withholding taxes** may apply:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Germany" icon="flag">
    5-10% withholding tax depending on double taxation treaties
  </Card>
  <Card title="Austria" icon="flag">
    0-10% depending on contracting country
  </Card>
  <Card title="Switzerland" icon="flag">
    0-8% according to double taxation agreements
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
