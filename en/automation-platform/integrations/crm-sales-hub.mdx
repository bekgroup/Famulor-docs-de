## Why CRM Integration for Voice AI?

### 📊 Automatic Pipeline Management  
Every call is automatically logged in your CRM with correct deal stages, lead scores, and follow-up activities.

### 🎯 360° Customer View  
Combine phone insights with CRM data for comprehensive customer intelligence and personalized conversations.

Your CRM data is retrieved and updated during the conversation to enable contextual, data-driven sales talks.

### 📈 Performance Tracking Revolution  
Automatic capture of all sales activities for precise forecasting, team performance analysis, and ROI tracking.
