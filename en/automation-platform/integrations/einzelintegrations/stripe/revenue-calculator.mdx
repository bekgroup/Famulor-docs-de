# Stripe Revenue Calculator

Use this page as a lightweight framework to estimate the revenue contribution of Stripe automations triggered by calls.

## Calculation Logic

1. Measure monthly qualified calls.
2. Define conversion rate and average revenue per account.
3. Add expansion revenue (upgrades, add-ons) and subtract churn impact.

## Formula (simplified model)

`MRR = (Qualified Calls x Conversion Rate x ARPA) + Expansion MRR - Churn MRR`
