### When to Use Inbound Calls

- **Customer Support**: Handle incoming inquiries 24/7 without human agents
- **Local Presence**: Provide local phone numbers for different regions
- **Cost Efficiency**: Typically cheaper for local customers calling you
- **Toll-Free Options**: Offer free calling for customers (may have surcharges)

### When to Use Outbound Calls

- **Sales Campaigns**: Reach out to leads and prospects automatically
- **Appointment Reminders**: Send automated reminders to reduce no-shows
- **Collections**: Follow up on overdue payments or invoices
- **Customer Follow-Ups**: Post-purchase surveys and feedback collection

<Warning>
Outbound calls to international destinations or premium numbers can incur higher carrier fees. Always verify destination rates before launching large campaigns.
</Warning>
