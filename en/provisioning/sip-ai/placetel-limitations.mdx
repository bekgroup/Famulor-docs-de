# Placetel Limitations

<Note>
  Unfortunately, Placetel currently does not support static SIP trunks for integration with Famulor.
</Note>

## Why doesn't the integration work at the moment?

<Card
  title="Current Technical Limitation"
  icon="triangle-exclamation"
  color="red">
  **Placetel unfortunately does not support static SIP trunks at this time.**
  
  This means that a direct SIP integration between Placetel and Famulor is technically not possible currently.
</Card>

<Card
  title="Contact the provider directly"
  icon="phone"
  color="blue">
  **Important:** Please ask Placetel directly if they can offer static SIP trunks!
  
  
    - Many providers continuously develop their services
    - The feature might already be available or planned
    - Direct inquiry can lead to individual solutions
  
</Card>

## What does this mean for you?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
