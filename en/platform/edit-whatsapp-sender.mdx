## 1. Open your sender

1. In Formula, open **WhatsApp Senders**.
2. Make sure your WhatsApp Business profile is already connected.
3. Scroll to the right in the sender row and click **Edit**.

<Tip>
If your sender is not connected yet, first complete the setup flow in the WhatsApp setup guides.
</Tip>

## 2. Edit the business profile section

Scroll down to **Business Profile** and update:

- **Description**
- **Business address**
- **Business category**

## 3. Update branding

In the branding area, you can set:

- **Logo**
- **Banner**
- **Brand color**

## 4. Add links and legal information

You can add important profile links such as:

- **Privacy policy URL**
- **Terms and conditions URL**
- Additional public URLs

## 5. Add website and contact details

Complete your profile with:

- **Website**
- **Contact information** (for example email, phone, or other business contact fields)

After saving, these details are shown in your WhatsApp Business profile so customers can see your business information directly in WhatsApp.
