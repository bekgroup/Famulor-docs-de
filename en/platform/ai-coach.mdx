## The purple wand

The coach is the **purple wand** in the UI. Click it to open a **panel on the right** with the **AI Coach**.

You’ll get **recommendations** for **latency** and **voice quality**. Use the **green button** to **apply suggested fixes in one go**, then check whether the assistant sounds right.

## Troubleshooting

Under **troubleshooting**, you get help when something feels off — for example if a call sounds **cut off** or **ends too early**. The coach suggests the right **fix**; apply it with **Apply Fix**.

## Best settings (engine & use case)

The **best settings** section recommends which **engine type** and configuration fit **your scenario**.
