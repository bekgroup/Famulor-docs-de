## 1. Open Connectors and create your first connector

1. Go to **Connectors**.
2. Click to create your first connector.
3. Choose a channel (for example **Web Widget** or **WhatsApp**; Messenger may be added as an additional option).

## 2. Select channel and assign assistant

1. Select **Web Widget** (highlighted in blue) and click **Next**.
2. Assign the assistant you want to use for this connector.

## 3. Configure widget and create connector

1. In widget configuration, adjust your Web Widget settings.
2. Click **Create connector**.

## 4. Use embed code on your website

After creation, copy the **embed code** and paste it into your website.

You can also create **multiple Web Widgets** for different sections of your site.
