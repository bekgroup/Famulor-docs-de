## What you can see in the chat list

At the top, you’ll get a quick overview, such as:

- **All conversations**: Every conversation across your channels
- **Last 7 days**: Conversations from the past seven days
- **Messages**: The messages your AI sent
- **Average conversation duration**: How long chats take on average

Below that, you’ll find a list of your customer messages and conversations.

## Open a conversation

Click a conversation in the list to open the detail view.

## What you’ll find in conversation details

- **Message panel (right)**: Read through the full conversation.
- **Conversation details (left)**: See key metadata (e.g., channel, timestamps, duration).
- **Extracted variables**: If you enabled variables on your assistant, you can see which variables were extracted from the chat.
