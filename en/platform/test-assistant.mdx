## 1. Free test in the assistant editor

1. Open your assistant in **Edit** mode.
2. Scroll to the top and click the light-blue **Test Assistant** button.
3. A panel opens on the right where you can test your assistant behavior in detail.

This editor test does **not consume balance**.

## 2. Live call test

If you want a real call test, you can start:

- A **web call**
- A **direct phone call**

This test **does consume balance** because it is handled like a normal call.

<Warning>
Live calls are **billed per second** and count as a **full call**.
</Warning>
