To export your calls, go to **Call history** in the left navigation. From there, you can select specific calls and download them as an export.

## Step by step

1. Open **Call history**.
2. In the selection box, choose the calls you want to export.
3. Click **Export calls**.
4. Select which **fields / information** should be included.
5. Click **Export**.
