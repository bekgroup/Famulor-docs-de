## Key KPIs
- Reachability (% of leads contacted)
- Call duration (median)
- Appointment/Deal conversion
- Cost per contact/appointment/deal

## Analytics Approach

### 1. Define Goals & Thresholds
Set target values per segment (e.g., reachability 35–55%). Use internal baselines and external benchmarks.

### 2. Create Cohorts
Compare time windows, script variants, target groups, and assistant voices.

### 3. Decide & Iterate
Allocate volume to the best-performing combinations.

### Benchmarks & Studies
- SDR/outbound reports provide benchmarks for connect rates and meetings per rep
- Source: [The Bridge Group – SDR Metrics & Compensation Report](https://www.bridgegroupinc.com/)
- Additionally: HubSpot/Gong reports on outbound performance

## Brief Study: KPIs & Benchmarks
- **Data basis**: Bridge Group SDR reports, HubSpot/Gong benchmarks
- **Key points**:
  - Track funnel-wide: Connect → Meeting → Opportunity → Win; optimize at each stage
  - Cohort analyses reduce spurious correlations (e.g., time window vs. script)
- **Limitations**: Benchmarks vary significantly by industry/ACV
