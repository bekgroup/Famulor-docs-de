## ICP & Persona
- ICP: Size, Industry, Region, Technology Stack
- Persona: Role, Goals, Pain Points, Trigger Events

## Segmentation
- By Industry, Use Case, Funnel Stage, Intent Signals
- Maintain custom fields in leads and filter them in campaigns

## Messaging Guide
- Value proposition per segment (max. 2 sentences)
- Relevance question + next step
- Reuse snippets in scripts

<Info>
Social selling supports outbound: Buyers often research before responding to outreach.  
Source: IDC/LinkedIn reports on B2B buying behavior (summaries at LinkedIn Sales Blogs).
</Info>

## Brief Study: Target Audiences & Messaging
- **Data basis**: LinkedIn/IDC buying behavior, HubSpot outbound data  
- **Key statements**:
  - Segment-specific benefits + personalization increase response rates  
  - Combining voice + social/email expands reach across segments  
- **Limitations**: Heterogeneous methodologies of public reports
