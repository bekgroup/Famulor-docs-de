We want everyone to understand limits in plain language—no telecom jargon required.

## What you’ll see

Open **[Limits](https://app.famulor.de/limits)** in your dashboard. There you can track how many outbound calls you still have for today and when your counter resets.

![Limits overview in the Famulor app](/images/limits-outboundcalls.png)

## Outbound calls through Famulor (integrated carrier)

If you place outbound calls using **Famulor’s built-in carrier**, those calls share a **daily allowance**. A common default is **1,000 calls per day**—your screen always shows the number that applies to **your** workspace.

**What counts toward this limit**

- Calls from **numbers you got through Famulor** (integrated dedicated numbers).
- Calls where you use **your own number as caller ID**, once that number is set up correctly in the app.

**What resets**

The page shows when your allowance **resets** (on a rolling basis, roughly every **24 hours**). The exact time on screen is what matters for your account.

## What stays unlimited

Some things are **not** capped by that daily outbound allowance:

- **Inbound calls** — answering calls is unlimited on our side.
- **Outbound over your own SIP trunk** — traffic through **your** carrier follows **your** limits, not a Famulor daily outbound cap.

## Need more outbound capacity? Send a request

Yes. If your volume grows and you need a **higher daily limit**, tap **Request increase** on the Limits page. A short form opens—we use your answers to review the change quickly.

You’ll be asked for:

- **Requested daily limit** — the number of outbound calls per day you’re aiming for (required).
- **Why you need a higher limit** — a few sentences about your use case (required). Honest context helps us say yes faster.
- **Business website** — optional, but useful if we want to learn how you use Famulor.

Then choose **Submit request** or **Cancel**.

![Request Outbound Limit Increase form](/images/limits-request-increase.png)

We review requests manually. If something is unclear, we may reach out through your usual support channel.

## Prefer no daily cap on outbound?

You can connect **your own SIP trunk** (for example Twilio, Telnyx, Zadarma, or DIDLogic). Your carrier, your numbers, your billing—and outbound through that path isn’t limited by Famulor’s integrated daily quota. In the app, use **Connect SIP trunk**, or start here: [SIP integration](/en/provisioning/sip-ai/sip-integration).

<Tip>
Numbers and reset times change per account. Always trust what you see on [app.famulor.de/limits](https://app.famulor.de/limits).
</Tip>
